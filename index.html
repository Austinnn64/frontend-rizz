A letter written for you, with love

When I first saw you, it felt like I was gazing into eternity. The cosmic stardust that occupies the universe lives in your eyes. They shine like a serene and infinite ocean of water. Every time I see you, it feels like new stars have been created somewhere in the universe, and if I could build all those stars into a constellational galaxy, I would create one so that we could have all the time in the world to spend together.

♥   ♥   ♥

Your voice has a gentle cadence to it that I want to hear every day. There is serenity, softness, and warmth to it that bring me peace; there is something sweet about your voice, and I will always remember the way it sounds long after I have heard it.

♥   ♥   ♥

I never knew love was real until you came into my life. I thought it was something that only happened to other people. I felt your magic in my soul when I looked into your eyes and realized that love was true and real and magnificently beautiful.

♥   ♥   ♥

You are the person I admire, the person I cherish, and the person who makes the world around her feel brighter by simply existing.

Forever yours,

Desmond
